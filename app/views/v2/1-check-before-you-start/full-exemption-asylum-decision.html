Based on what you've told us

You get help with NHS costs and do not need to apply

Because you get Section 95 asylum support, you are entitled to help.

How to get your help

You will be issued with an HC2 certificate by the NHS Business Services Authority. This will be sent with 21 days of you receiving your support letter from UK Visas and Immigration(UKVI).

Your certificate lasts for 6 months. You will get a renewed certificate after this if you are still getting Section 95 asylum support.

If you do not have an HC2

If you do not have an HC2 or it has expired, you need to contact Migrant Help UK on 0808 801 0503.

What you'll get

You get free:

  • NHS prescriptions
  • NHS dental check-ups and treatment
  • sight tests
  • NHS wigs and fabric supports

You get money off:

  • new glasses or contact lenses
  • repairing or replacing your glasses or contact lenses
  • travel for NHS treatment
