Check if you can apply online for help with NHS costs

We will now ask you 7 questions to make sure you can apply online at this time. As the service is still being developed, not everyone is currently able to apply online.

If you cannot apply online yet, you can still use the paper form, called an HC1.

{% call details({ summaryText: "How to get an HC1 form" }) %}

You can get an HC1 form:

  • a local pharmacy
  • your GP practice
  • a local Jobcentre
  • Citizens Advice
